decode.plot.functions.plot_spectrum(cube, xtick, ytick, aperture, ax=None, **kwargs)[source]¶

Plot a spectrum.

Parameters

  • cube (xarray.DataArray) – Cube which the spectrum information is included.

  • xtick (str) – Type of x axis. ‘freq’: Frequency [GHz]. ‘id’: Kid id.

  • ytick (str) – Type of y axis. ‘max’: Maximum. ‘sum’: Summation. ‘mean’: Mean.

  • aperture (str) – The shape of aperture. ‘box’: Box. ‘circle’: Circle.

  • ax (matplotlib.axes) – Axis you want to plot on.

  • kwargs (optional) –

    When ‘box’ is specified as shape,

    xc: Center of x. yc: Center of y. width: Width. height: Height. xmin: Minimum of x. xmax: Maximum of x. ymin: Minimum of y. ymax: Maximum of y.

    When ‘circle’ is specified as shape,

    xc: Center of x. yc: Center of y. radius: Radius.

    Remaining kwargs are passed to ax.step().

Notes

All kwargs should be specified as pixel coordinates.
